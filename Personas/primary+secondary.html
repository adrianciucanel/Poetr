Nastase Elena is a 43 Romanian poet. Educated in Bucharest, she went on to receive a degree in philology from the University of Bucharest, starting a grade school teacher career that lasted almost ten years, first in a village along the Danube, then in Bucharest. With her first book won the Romanian Writers Union Prize for a debut in poetry.

She knows how useful can be a computer and that's why she bought one, but has a bit of problem by using it. Her son, Dragos teached her how to use it: explain how to get for informations from the internet, how to be organized on the computer and how to write an save the documents. Elena learn quick and now she use the computer alone. There are moments when she have writer's block and use the internet for informations and moments when she have a lot of ideeas but cannot keystroke fast enought and lose some of that.
