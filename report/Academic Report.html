Abstract

Poetry is a form of literature that uses aesthetic and rhytmic qualities of language - such as phonaesthetics, sound symbolism, and metre - to evoke meanings is addition to, or in place of, the prosaic ostensible meaning. Poetry has a long history dating back to the Sumerian Epic of Gilgamesh. Early poems evolved from folk songs such as the Chinese Shijing, or from a need to retell oral epics.

Poetry uses forms and conventions to suggest differential interpretation to words, or to evoke emotive responses. Devices such as assonance, alliteration, onomatopoeia and rhythm are sometimes used to achieve musical or incantatory effects. The use of ambiguisty, symbolism, irony, and other stylistic elements of poetic diction often leaves a poem open to multiple interpretations.

Some poetry types are specific to particular cultures and genres and respond to charasteristics of the language in wich the poet writes. Readers accustomed to identifying poetry with Dante, Goethe, Mickiewicz and Rumi may think of it as written in lines based on rhyme and regular meter; there are, however, traditions that use other means to create rhythm an euphony. Much modern poetry reflects a critique of poetic tradition, playing with and testing, among other things, the principle of euphony itself, sometimes altogether forgoing rhyme or set rhythm. In today's increasing globalized world, poets often adapt form styles and techiques from diverse cultures and languages.

Definition

EasyPoetry aims to be a useful and easy for both casual users who want to write some lyrics and intensive users. It includes a text editor where the clients can easily write poems, receive appropriate rhymes, examine the definition of a term, keep and manage writings, share poems on social networks, create an pleasant and friendly environment for the act of creation.

Users targeted

  • writers who compose poems;
  • students at the universities of literary profile;
  • educators and teachers who work with children;
  • parents who use rhymes as method of learning and inspire values;
  • people in love who want to express their feelings in chosen words;

Project

We looked through the internet to find applications that have a similar profile. We found several applications that offer interesting features and a number of sites offering rhymes based on a search word, mostly in english language. There are a few sites that offer rhymes in romanian language (rimeaza.org).

Initially, we intended to make an application in the Romanian language. We found a free dictionary in sqlite database format with words and explanations la dexonline.ro. We abandoned the idea because the contents had to be processed( the words have accents, not always respect the same format, we have to establish declination rules, how to divide into syllables etc.) which would have taken some time.

We found instead a free API in English which offering to a number of requests per day a lot of facilities: definitions of words, rhymes , synonyms , antonyms , words with similar meaning , number of syllables.
